Biome

Personal Rating

Overview

"Protect your baby animals and build the most biodiverse ecosystem in Biome, the ultimate nature themed tableau builder."

Game experience

Biome is a combination of stratetic card placements and engine building in a nature themed tableau builder.
After learning the basic rules and playing the game a few times, I enjoy on one hand the easy game play but also the complexity of possibilties to build your biome.

Recommendation

I highly recommend the game, if you are already used to tableau building games.
